# Privacy Policy — Markdown Reader Extension

Last updated: April 6, 2026

## Summary
Markdown Reader does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data whatsoever.

## Data Collection
This extension collects NO personal data of any kind:
- No browsing history
- No personal information
- No analytics or telemetry
- No cookies

## How it works
All Markdown rendering happens entirely locally on your device. 
The extension only reads .md and .markdown files that you explicitly open in your browser.
No data ever leaves your computer.
